LETTER FROM THE SECRETARY GENERAL

Dear Delegates,


It is my great pleasure to present to you the 11th edition of BITSMUN to be held from 29th to 31st March, 2019.

Boasting to be one of the oldest college conferences in India, we at BITSMUN Pilani pride ourselves on giving delegates the best experience possible. Since its inception, BITSMUN Pilani has had delegates not only from India but also from other countries like the United States, Pakistan, Bangladesh, Nepal, Bhutan, bringing over 300 engaging minds together. Our aim has always been to generate interest and concern about problems and issues that infect the world so that today’s youth can learn to solve tomorrow’s problems.

BITSMUN 2019 will have 5 committees whose agendas are both relevent and interesting. With a dynamic and experienced Executive Board, an exciting experience is assured. This year BITSMUN will also have a series of talks by eminent personalities from different walks of life. In addition to these talks, there are several events planned to enhance your experience with us. We look forward to hosting you for three days of intense debate, stimulating discussions and shrewd diplomacy, in what we hope turns out to be one of the most memorable experiences of your life.

Regards,

Niranjan Jahagirdar

Secretary-General

WHY JOIN US

Oldest College MUN Conference

BITSMUN is the oldest college MUN conference in India. The conference was founded in 2007, and BITSMUN 2019 will mark its tenth edition. Over the years, the conference has attracted college and school delegates from over 15 countries and all across India. The college is also famous for its humble yet iconic campus, and that itself is an experience of its own.

Experienced Executive Board

The BITSMUN society has always emphasized on good debating quality. To ensure this, our executive board is carefully picked from amongst the best MUNners in the country.

Good Cause

The proceeds from BITSMUN 2019 will be donated for a good cause, after expending towards the necessities for the event. If you have a cause you are passionate about and want us to donate towards, please mail us at secretary.general@bitsmun.co.in
